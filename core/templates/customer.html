Welcome to FoodieFreak, Customers!

FoodieFreak enhances your dining experience by allowing you to browse, order, and track your meals directly from your table. No more queues, just convenience and flavor!

Customer Features

  • Customer Dashboard: View current orders, order history, and wallet balance (if available).
  • Table Identification: Select or enter your table number to start ordering.
  • Browse Menu: Explore food items, filter by category, and read item details.
  • Place Orders:
    • Add multiple items to your cart
    • Specify quantity and add special requests
    • Place order for your table with one click
  • Order Tracking: Track real-time status updates (Pending, Preparing, Ready, Served).
  • Payment (Optional):
    • Pay via wallet, card, or other online options
    • Top-up your wallet anytime
  • Order History: View past orders and quickly reorder your favorites.
